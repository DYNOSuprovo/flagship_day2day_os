7-Day Indian Diet Plan for Weight Loss (Approx 1200 Calories)

Day 1:
Breakfast: Poha with vegetables + 1 cup tea (no sugar)
Lunch: 1 Roti + Dal Tadka + Salad
Dinner: Vegetable Soup + Grilled Paneer

Day 2:
Breakfast: Oats Upma with veggies
Lunch: Brown Rice + Rajma + Curd
Dinner: Mixed Vegetable Sabzi + 1 Roti

Day 3:
Breakfast: Idli (2) + Sambar
Lunch: Vegetable Khichdi + Raita
Dinner: Chicken/Paneer Salad

Day 4:
Breakfast: Besan Chilla (2)
Lunch: 1 Roti + Palak Paneer
Dinner: Lentil Soup

Day 5:
Breakfast: Dalia (Porridge) with milk/veggies
Lunch: Brown Rice + Fish Curry / Soya Chunks
Dinner: Sauteed Vegetables

Day 6:
Breakfast: Multigrain Toast + Egg/Paneer Bhurji
Lunch: 1 Roti + Baingan Bharta
Dinner: Clear Soup + Grilled Chicken/Tofu

Day 7:
Breakfast: Masala Oats
Lunch: Vegetable Biryani (small portion) + Raita
Dinner: Fruit Salad + Milk
